ABOUT ME

I’ve always been curious about how people interact with technology. Growing up, I loved solving puzzles, so when I was introduced to programming, it immediately clicked. I saw coding challenges as puzzles waiting to be solved, and that process felt incredibly rewarding. When I later discovered UI/UX, everything started to come together. I realized that great design isn’t just about aesthetics. It’s about how something works, how it makes someone feel, and how effortlessly it fits into their life. I want to create experiences that fit those criteria.

I’m from Philadelphia, PA, so choosing Drexel made a lot of sense. It’s local, which means I get to stay close to my family, but it also offers amazing opportunities through its co-op program. On top of that, I love being in a major city and everything that comes with it, from the energy and diversity to the constant inspiration that comes from being surrounded by new ideas and experiences.

Outside of design and school, I’m really into video games. I’ve competed in Valorant at a high level, and more recently, I’ve gotten into League of Legends. It’s a whole new kind of challenge, and I’m enjoying diving into it. I also love exploring the city. I would consider myself to be pretty open minded, as I really enjoy trying new things. I think staying curious and open to new experiences helps me grow, both as a person and as a designer.
